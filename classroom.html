Classroom Classes

Our classroom classes offer a chance to play, meet other children and develop new skills along the way.
Our weekly engaging social play classes offer a wide variety of experiences tailored to the needs of each age group.
As well as a chance for children socialize with children of their own age,
it also offers parents/caregivers the opportunity to get to know others.

A great way to spend quality time with your little one.

Schedule of Classes:

Saturday and Sunday:
1000AM – 1100AM
1200Noon – 0100P
0300PM – 0400PM

Price per Session:

€15.00 per session or €120.00 for 10 sessions.

We really try to make it feel like home, and go out of our way
to make sure our activities are as interesting to the big people as the little ones,
each child receives their own set of play tools and our venues are clean, comfortable, safe and accessible.
We're a little obsessed about cleanliness... well with that many little ones sharing our space we need to be.
Small equipment is washed daily with the larger items such as the flooring and yoga mats weekly.

Our Classroom Instructors are group of early childhood experts,
Montessori teachers and music teachers.
They are all trained in first aid and emergency response.

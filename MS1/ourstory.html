Our Story

It all started a year ago when a group of friends and fellow parents met up one Sunday afternoon for a lunch. We all had our babies and toddlers along so it was a fun yet chaotic day. Amidst all the chaos, we ended up discussing some of our challenges in finding a meaningful yet fun place where our kids can enjoy their weekend. There were plenty of activities we can chose from however most of which are on separate locations. With this in mind, we’ve decided to create a wonderful centre with different activities that every parent can chose for their lovable babies.

We’ve teamed up with other parents who are professional in their field of interest. We have parents who are professional swimmers/coaches, football coaches, early development experts and Montessori teachers. And with all our collective efforts, we always maintain a safe and healthy environment for our little angels.

Class Program Overview

All of our classes are group classes where in parent/s participation is highly encouraged. We have 3 classes to offer depending on your little one’s age and level of development.

Our Swimming program is catered for infant up to 6 months old, Classroom Playlab is for infant 4 months up to 12 months and Ballgames is ideally from 8 months up to 24 months.

For the safety and quality of our classes, we are setting a maximum of 8 little angels per class.

We understand that safety is the main concern for all parents like us so we made sure that our centre complies to all safety regulation set by the governing bodies. Our facilities are cleaned every session and disinfected every end of the day.
